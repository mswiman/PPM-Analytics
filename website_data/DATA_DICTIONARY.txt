
================================================================================
DATA DICTIONARY
================================================================================

PLAYER IDENTIFICATION
---------------------
name            Player's full name
player_id       Internal player ID
nba_id          Official NBA.com player ID
season          Season year (e.g., 2024 = 2023-24 season)

RAPM METRICS (per 100 possessions)
-----------------------------------
o_rapm          Offensive RAPM (points added on offense)
d_rapm          Defensive RAPM (points prevented on defense)
                NOTE: NEGATIVE d_rapm = GOOD defense
total_rapm      Total RAPM = o_rapm - d_rapm (positive = better)

my_o_rapm       My calculated O-RAPM (for comparison)
my_d_rapm       My calculated D-RAPM
my_total_rapm   My calculated Total RAPM

josh_o_rapm     Josh's 3-year rolling O-RAPM (ground truth)
josh_d_rapm     Josh's 3-year rolling D-RAPM

PLAYING TIME
------------
games           Games played in season
off_poss        Offensive possessions played
def_poss        Defensive possessions played
minutes         Total minutes played

TRACKING STATS
--------------
drives                  Total drives to basket
drive_rate              Drives per touch (0-1)
drive_fg_pct            FG% on drives (0-1)
secondary_ast_per_100   Secondary assists per 100 possessions
potential_ast_per_100   Potential assists per 100 possessions
adj_ast_per_100         Adjusted assists per 100 possessions
rim_dfg                 Rim FG% allowed on defense (0-1)
                        NOTE: HIGHER = worse defense
dreb_contest_rate       Contested rebound % (0-1)
blocks                  Total blocks
steals                  Total steals
touches                 Total touches

CAREER-WEIGHTED FEATURES
------------------------
o_rapm_career_wt        Career-weighted O-RAPM (recency bias)
d_rapm_career_wt        Career-weighted D-RAPM

MATCHUP FEATURES
----------------
o_pts_vs_exp_elite      Points vs expected vs elite defenders
o_poss_vs_elite         Possessions vs elite defenders
o_n_elite_faced         Number of elite defenders faced
d_pts_vs_exp_elite      Points vs expected vs elite scorers
d_poss_vs_elite         Possessions vs elite scorers
d_n_elite_guarded       Number of elite scorers guarded

PROJECTION TARGETS
------------------
o_rapm_1yr              O-RAPM one year from now
o_rapm_3yr              O-RAPM three years from now
o_rapm_5yr              O-RAPM five years from now
o_rapm_7yr              O-RAPM seven years from now
o_rapm_5_7yr            Average O-RAPM over years 5-7
(same pattern for d_rapm and total_rapm)

THRESHOLDS
----------
Elite O-RAPM:           > +1.5 (elite scorer)
Elite D-RAPM:           < -1.5 (elite defender)
Min possessions:        500 for reliable RAPM
Min matchup poss:       20 per individual matchup
